Welcome to mridata.org!

mridata.org is an open platform for researchers to share magnetic resonance imaging (MRI) raw k-space datasets. The website is designed to facilitate sharing MRI datasets from different vendors, with features including automatic ISMRMRD conversion, parameter extraction and thumbnail generation.

To start, you can browse projects contributed by users below. To view all available datasets, we refer you to our Data List. After registering as a user, you can upload datasets from GE, Philips and Siemens. To know more about using the website, we refer you to our FAQ. To visit our old website, please visit old.mridata.org.
